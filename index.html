About BSides PDX

BSides Portland is a tax-exempt charitable 501(c)(3) organization founded with the mission to cultivate the Pacific Northwest information security and hacking community by creating local inclusive opportunities for learning, networking, collaboration, and teaching. If you have questions, comments, or are interested in being more involved, don't hesitate to reach out to info [@] bsidespdx.org.
